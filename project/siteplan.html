Site Plan for Water Gardening

Site Name

Harrisonburg Water Gardens

This name represents a hub for all things related to water gardening.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for water gardening enthusiasts by providing plant and equipment recommendations and maintenance tips.

Scenarios

  • What are the best plants for a water garden in a temperate climate?
  • How do I maintain a healthy water garden during winter?

Color Schema

Primary color: #006400 for headings (Dark Green)

Secondary color: #8FBC8F for background (Dark Sea Green)

Typography

Primary font: Arial for headings

Secondary font: Verdana for body text
